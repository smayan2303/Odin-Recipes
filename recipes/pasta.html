Pasta

Description

This recipe is very easy to make and not time consuming either. Make sure to give it a try!

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • oil
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Let the dough rest
  4. Divide the dough into balls
  5. Use a machine to cut and roll the dough
